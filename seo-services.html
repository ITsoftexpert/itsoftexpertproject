Frequently Answered Questions

Everything You’ve Wanted to Know About SEO (But Were Too Afraid to Ask)

How long does SEO take to work?

It’s a marathon, not a sprint – expect noticeable results in 3-6 months.

Do I need SEO if I already use ads?

Absolutely! SEO is the long-term game changer, while ads give you a short-term boost.

What if I don’t rank on page one?

Page one is where 75% of clicks happen. We work to get you there, improving your chances of driving traffic and leads.

Why does SEO cost money if it’s organic?

While you’re not paying for clicks like ads, SEO involves significant expertise, tools, and time to optimise your site and achieve results.

How do I track my SEO progress?

We provide detailed reports on traffic, keyword rankings, and performance metrics, so you’ll always know where you stand.

Is SEO a one-time effort?

Nope! SEO is ongoing. Algorithms change, competitors evolve, and your business grows staying on top requires regular updates.
