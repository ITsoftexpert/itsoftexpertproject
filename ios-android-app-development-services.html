Frequently Answered Questions

iOS & Android Demystified

Which technologies do you use for mobile app development?

We use Swift & Objective-C for iOS, Kotlin & Java for Android, and Flutter, React Native, or Ionic for cross-platform apps, depending on project requirements.

Should I build a native app or a cross-platform app?

If you want high performance and platform-specific features, go for native apps. If you need a cost-effective and faster development cycle, cross-platform solutions like Flutter or React Native are great choices.

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

The timeline varies based on complexity. A basic app can take 2-3 months, while a feature-rich, custom app may take 6 months or more.

Will my app be optimized for performance and security?

Yes! We ensure fast load times, smooth performance, data encryption, and compliance with security standards like GDPR and HIPAA.

Can you integrate third-party services and APIs into my app?

Absolutely! We integrate payment gateways, social logins, CRMs, analytics tools, and custom APIs as per your requirements.
